Our Mission

We support sustainable businesses by meeting their demands for bulk raw materials and help small-scale farmers to manage waste products in an efficient way. We make processing and delivery a hassle free experience and add value to agricultural by-products. We aim to help the farmers of India profit from a sustainable waste management scheme.

Our Team

WEasy is a collaborative project of students of Chitkara University Institute of Technology. Our team is working constantly to make WEeasy better each day and we plan to make WEasy a reality soon.

Why WEasy?

Our amazing team made WEasy with diligence and our passion is fueled by the enthusiam for providing eco-friendly waste management and recycling. We process agro-waste and tranform it into useful material such as:-


Banana Fabric

Made from banana peels
Hay Fabric

Made from discarded hay
Bird Nest

Made from fertiliser containers
Compost Manure

Made from rotten fruits and vegetables
Rice Straw Bags

Made from discarded rice
